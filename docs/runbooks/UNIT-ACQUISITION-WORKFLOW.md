# Runbook — Unit Acquisition Workflow

**Owner:** Owner / Maintenance / Accounting
**Frequency:** Per acquired tractor/trailer
**Systems:** IH35 TMS (Fleet/Maintenance, Accounting, Safety), Samsara

Bring a newly acquired unit from purchase to in-service and assigned.

## Steps

1. **Purchase decision + financing.** Confirm purchase price, financing/lease
   terms, and record the liability/loan in Accounting if financed.
2. **Title transfer + DOT registration.** Complete title work; register the unit
   and record the DOT/registration details.
3. **Insurance binder.** Add the unit to the policy; confirm the binder/COI before
   the unit moves.
4. **Create the unit record.** Fleet/Maintenance → add unit (unit number, VIN,
   make/model/year, plates, ownership/lease company).
5. **Samsara device install.** Install/pair the ELD/gateway and confirm telematics
   reporting in.
6. **Initial maintenance schedule.** Set up PM schedule and any inspection/warranty
   tracking for the unit.
7. **Set status to In-Service** once compliant and ready.
8. **Assign to a driver** (or pool) and confirm the assignment shows in dispatch.

## Verification

- [ ] Financing/liability recorded (if applicable).
- [ ] Title + DOT registration complete.
- [ ] Insurance binder/COI on file.
- [ ] Unit record created; plates + ownership set.
- [ ] Samsara device paired and reporting.
- [ ] PM schedule initialized.
- [ ] Status In-Service; driver assigned.

## Escalation

Do not dispatch a unit before the insurance binder is confirmed — escalate to the
Owner if coverage is pending.
