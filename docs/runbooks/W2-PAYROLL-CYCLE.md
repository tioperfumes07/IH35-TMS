# Runbook — W-2 Payroll Cycle (Office Staff)

**Owner:** Accounting / Owner
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly or monthly
**Systems:** QuickBooks Online Payroll, IH35 TMS (Accounting)

Run payroll for W-2 office employees through QBO Payroll.

## Steps

1. **Confirm the pay period** and pay date on the calendar.
2. **Pull timecards** (if hourly staff) and confirm hours with managers.
3. **Run QBO Payroll** for office staff:
   - Enter/confirm hours and salaries.
   - Apply approved bonuses or reimbursements.
4. **Verify withholdings + benefits** — federal/state tax, FICA, health, retirement.
5. **Review the payroll preview** totals against budget before submitting.
6. **Submit payroll** in QBO Payroll.
7. **Distribute paystubs** (QBO emails employees; confirm delivery).
8. **Confirm tax payments** are scheduled/handled by QBO Payroll.
9. **Confirm the payroll JE** posted to the correct GL accounts in QBO.
10. **Reconcile the payroll debit** in Banking once it clears.

## Verification

- [ ] Hours/salaries confirmed.
- [ ] Withholdings and benefits correct.
- [ ] Payroll submitted; paystubs delivered.
- [ ] Tax payments scheduled.
- [ ] Payroll bank debit reconciled.

## Notes

- Driver pay is **not** handled here — see [1099-CONTRACTOR-PAYROLL.md](./1099-CONTRACTOR-PAYROLL.md)
  for contractor driver settlements.
