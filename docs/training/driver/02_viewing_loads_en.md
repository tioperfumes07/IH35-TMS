# View Your Loads

## Open the app
Step 1: Open IH35 Driver app.  
Step 2: Login if needed.  
Step 3: Tap **Loads**.  
Step 4: Check today's loads first.

## Open load detail
Step 1: Tap a load card.  
Step 2: Review pickup location and time.  
Step 3: Review delivery location and time.  
Step 4: Read dispatch notes.

## During your shift
Step 1: Refresh if a new load is missing.  
Step 2: Watch status updates.  
Step 3: Confirm you are on the right load before check-in.

## If something looks wrong
Step 1: Take screenshot.  
Step 2: Call dispatch.  
Step 3: Share load number and what looks wrong.
