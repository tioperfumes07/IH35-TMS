# Install IH35 Driver App

## iPhone (Safari)
Step 1: Open Safari.  
Step 2: Type `driver.ih35dispatch.com`.  
Step 3: Tap Login.  
Step 4: Sign in.  
Step 5: Tap Share (square with arrow).  
Step 6: Tap **Add to Home Screen**.  
Step 7: Tap **Add**.  
Step 8: Open the new IH35 Driver icon.

## Android (Chrome)
Step 1: Open Chrome.  
Step 2: Type `driver.ih35dispatch.com`.  
Step 3: Tap Login and sign in.  
Step 4: Tap the 3-dot menu.  
Step 5: Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.  
Step 6: Confirm install.  
Step 7: Open app from your home screen.

## Quick check
Step 1: Open the app icon.  
Step 2: Confirm your dashboard loads.  
Step 3: Keep camera and location permissions on.

## If something breaks
Step 1: Check internet signal.  
Step 2: Close and reopen app.  
Step 3: Try login again.  
Step 4: Call dispatch support if still blocked.
