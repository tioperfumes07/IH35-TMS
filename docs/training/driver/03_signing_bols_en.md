# Sign BOLs and Upload Photos

## Pickup signature
Step 1: Open active load.  
Step 2: Tap pickup stop.  
Step 3: Tap **Sign**.  
Step 4: Ask shipper contact to sign.  
Step 5: Tap Save.

## Delivery signature
Step 1: Open delivery stop.  
Step 2: Tap **Sign**.  
Step 3: Ask receiver to sign.  
Step 4: Confirm name if asked.  
Step 5: Tap Save.

## Upload photos
Step 1: Tap **Upload Photo**.  
Step 2: Take clear photos.  
Step 3: Make sure text is readable.  
Step 4: Upload and wait for confirmation.

## If upload fails
Step 1: Check signal and retry.  
Step 2: Retake blurry photo.  
Step 3: Call dispatch if still failing.
