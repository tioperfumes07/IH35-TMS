# Report Issues Fast

## Mechanical issue
Step 1: Open active load.  
Step 2: Tap **Report Issue**.  
Step 3: Choose Mechanical issue.  
Step 4: Add short notes and photos.  
Step 5: Send and call dispatch.

## Accident
Step 1: Put safety first.  
Step 2: Tap **Report Issue** and choose Accident.  
Step 3: Add location, notes, photos.  
Step 4: Send report.  
Step 5: Call safety/dispatch immediately.

## Cannot deliver
Step 1: Tap **Report Issue**.  
Step 2: Choose Cannot deliver.  
Step 3: Add reason and location.  
Step 4: Send and wait for instructions.

## Other issue
Step 1: Choose **Other**.  
Step 2: Write clear notes.  
Step 3: Add photo evidence if possible.  
Step 4: Call dispatch if urgent.
