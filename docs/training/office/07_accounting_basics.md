## Common questions
**Q: Is invoice creation always instant?**  
A: Usually fast, but sync timing can vary. Always verify posted status.

**Q: What if QBO sync stays pending too long?**  
A: Escalate with invoice ID, status, and timestamp.

**Q: Can we edit posted invoices directly?**  
A: Follow accounting controls and approval policy before edits.

## What to do if something breaks
- Retry once and capture exact error text.
- Cross-check dashboard totals against source transactions.
- Escalate repeated sync failures to accounting/support with IDs.
- Do not create manual duplicates as a workaround.
