## Common questions
**Q: A line item is disputed after preview. Now what?**  
A: Pause finalization and route to dispute workflow before posting final outcome.

**Q: Can one person approve and acknowledge?**  
A: Follow role controls and local policy.

**Q: Net pay looks off. What should I check first?**  
A: Recheck deductions, adjustments, and date range in scope.

## What to do if something breaks
- If preview fails, retry once and capture exact error.
- If totals look wrong, compare line-by-line against source records.
- If acknowledgment is missing, resend and verify channel.
- Escalate blockers with settlement ID and timestamp.
