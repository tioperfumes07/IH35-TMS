# Managing Customers

## What you'll learn
- How to create a new customer profile correctly
- Which required fields must be complete
- How detention/free time and factoring settings affect operations
- How FMCSA verification appears in the workflow

## Before you start
- You need permission to create/edit customers
- Gather MC#, billing contact, and terms from onboarding
- Confirm approved credit limit and payment terms

## Common questions
**Q: Can dispatch start before billing fields are complete?**  
A: Avoid that. Missing billing data usually causes invoicing delays and rework.

**Q: Who sets payment terms and credit limit?**  
A: Follow your office approval policy; those are controlled business settings.

**Q: FMCSA is pending. Can we continue?**  
A: Escalate according to policy before high-risk activity.

## What to do if something breaks
- If save fails, verify required fields and formats.
- If FMCSA status does not update, escalate with customer ID.
- If factoring config looks incorrect, pause and confirm with accounting.
- If permissions fail, ask supervisor/admin for role review.
