# Safety

The Safety module (SAFETY) is the carrier's compliance and accountability hub. It holds driver files, training, hours-of-service compliance, inspections, incidents, fines, and the documents that prove the fleet is operating legally.

## Overview
Safety is organized as a set of tabs grouped by area, navigated from the top hover-dropdown menu. The groups are:

- **Driver Files & Training** — Driver Files, Drug & Alcohol, Safety Meetings.
- **Hours & Fatigue** — Hours of Service, HOS Violations.
- **Inspections & FMCSA** — Vehicle Inspections (IDVR), DOT Inspections, CSA Score, DOT Compliance.
- **Incidents & Claims** — Accidents & Incidents, Damage Reports, Trailer Interchanges, Cargo Claims.
- **Fines & Discipline** — Internal Fines, External Fines, Complaints (visible to Owner/Admin/Safety only).
- **Driver Financial Safety** — Escrow Record.
- **Compliance Docs & Monitoring** — Insurance, Permits, Integrity Reports.
- **Settings.**

## Key tasks
- **Keep driver files current** — track licenses, medical cards, drug & alcohol tests, and training completions per driver.
- **Monitor hours of service** — review HOS status and follow up on violations.
- **Log incidents and claims** — record accidents, damage, trailer interchanges, and cargo claims as they happen.
- **Track fines and complaints** — internal and external fines, plus privacy-gated complaints.
- **Maintain compliance documents** — insurance, permits, and integrity monitoring.

## Tips & gotchas
- Safety records are institutional memory: history is append-only, so entries are added and corrected, never erased.
- Complaints are privacy-gated — only Owner, Administrator, and Safety roles can see them.
- The default driver filter favors recently active drivers; widen it when you need the full roster.
- Safety navigation is always the top hover-dropdown, never a left side panel.

## FAQ
- **Who can see complaints?** Only Owner, Administrator, and Safety roles.
- **Can I delete a safety record?** No — safety and accountability records are permanent institutional memory.
- **Where are escrow balances?** Under Driver Financial Safety → Escrow Record.
