# Drivers

The Drivers module (DRIVERS) is the roster and profile system for every driver in the fleet. It is where drivers are created, kept current, deactivated when they leave, and where their pay and hours connect to the rest of the system.

## Overview
Each driver has a profile with their identity, license, contact details, and status. Driver records connect outward to settlements (pay), hours of service (compliance), and dispatch (who can be assigned to a load).

## Key tasks
- **Create a driver** — use **+ Create** to add a new driver profile with name, license, and contact info.
- **Deactivate a driver** — when a driver leaves, deactivate the profile. This removes them from active lists and disables their app login while preserving their full history.
- **Reactivate a driver** — a deactivated driver can be brought back, which restores their active status and app access (a terminated driver stays terminated).
- **Hide or show a driver in lists** — a reversible visibility toggle on the driver profile lets you hide a driver from working lists without deactivating them, and show them again at any time.
- **Review pay and hours** — a driver's settlements and HOS clocks are reachable from their profile.

## Tips & gotchas
- **Deactivate vs. hide:** deactivating ends a driver's active status and app login; hiding only removes them from list views and is fully reversible. Pick the one that matches the situation.
- Driver lists default to **Active** drivers; switch the status filter to see inactive or all.
- Driver records are never deleted — a departed driver is deactivated and archived so settlements and safety history stay intact.

## FAQ
- **A driver came back — do I make a new profile?** No. Reactivate the existing profile so their history stays connected.
- **What's the difference between hiding and deactivating?** Hiding just removes the driver from lists (reversible); deactivating also disables their login and active status.
- **Why is a driver missing from the list?** They may be deactivated or hidden — change the status filter or check the visibility toggle on their profile.
