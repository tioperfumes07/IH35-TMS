# USMCA Driver Onboarding Guide (English)

**Carrier:** USMCA LLC  
**Effective:** July 2026  
**Audience:** Drivers newly assigned to the USMCA carrier entity

---

## 1. Pay Structure Under USMCA

Your pay is calculated per-mile (or percentage) under the **USMCA carrier code** — distinct from TRANSP. Settlements appear in the TMS portal under the USMCA operating company. QBO class assignment is `UNIT-DRIVER`.

## 2. PWA Login

- URL: same TMS portal — select **USMCA** from the company dropdown after login.
- Your login credentials remain unchanged.
- Contact dispatch if your USMCA carrier doesn't appear — IT provisioning may be pending.

## 3. Border Crossing Protocols

USMCA-specific crossings require:
- Valid C-TPAT pre-clearance number (obtain from Safety team)
- CTMX trailer seal number entered in the app before departure
- Lane selection: use the TMS **Border Crossing Wizard** → select USMCA carrier
- POD upload required within 2 hours of delivery on USMCA loads

## 4. Document Requirements

| Document | Where to upload |
|----------|-----------------|
| Mexican Commercial Driver License (LCF) | Safety → Driver Files |
| Customs broker authorization letter | Dispatch → Load documents |
| COI showing USMCA coverage | Safety → Insurance |

## 5. Settlement Timing & QBO Mapping

- Settlements paid weekly on Fridays (same as TRANSP).
- Your W-9/1099 form must be on file under the USMCA entity (not TRANSP).
- Contact accounting at ext. 204 to update.
